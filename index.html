About Garg Medical Agencies

Established in 2002, Garg Medical Agencies has been a trusted name in veterinary pharmaceutical services for over 20 years. Located in the heart of Mathura, Uttar Pradesh, we serve farmers, veterinarians, pet owners, and livestock businesses across India.

As a licensed veterinary pharmacy, we are committed to providing high-quality medicines, vaccines, and supplements for all types of animals. Our team of qualified veterinary professionals ensures that every product meets the highest standards of quality and efficacy.

We pride ourselves on our extensive range of trusted veterinary brands and our ability to serve both wholesale and retail customers with professionalism and integrity.
