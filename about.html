About Garg Medical Agencies

Trusted Veterinary Pharmacy Since 2002

Our Story

Garg Medical Agencies was established in 2002 with a simple mission: to provide high-quality veterinary medicines and supplements to farmers, veterinarians, and pet owners across India. What started as a small pharmacy in Mathura has grown into a trusted name in the veterinary pharmaceutical industry.

For over 20 years, we have been committed to serving the animal health community with integrity, professionalism, and a deep understanding of the unique needs of farmers, livestock owners, and veterinary professionals.

Our success is built on the foundation of trust, quality, and customer satisfaction. We take pride in being a licensed veterinary pharmacy that maintains the highest standards of pharmaceutical practice.

Our Journey

2002 - Establishment

Garg Medical Agencies was founded in Mathura with a vision to serve the veterinary community with quality medicines and professional support.

2005 - License & Expansion

Obtained full veterinary pharmacy license and expanded product range to serve more animals and regions.

2010 - Pan-India Reach

Established wholesale and retail supply network across India, serving farmers and veterinary clinics nationwide.

2020 - Digital Integration

Embraced digital channels including WhatsApp ordering and online communication for better customer service.

2024 - Present

Continuing to serve with 20+ years of experience and commitment to animal health and veterinary care.
